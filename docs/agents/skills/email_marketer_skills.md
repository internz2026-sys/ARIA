# Email Marketer Skills

## Subject Line Formulas

**Optimal:** 30-50 chars / 6-10 words. Mobile cutoff: ~35-40 chars.

**Boosters:** Numbers (+open AND reply), first name (+20-26% open), 1 emoji (39% open rate), urgency words (+22%).

1. `The [topic] mistake that costs you [result]`
2. `[Number] ways to [outcome] this week`
3. `Are you still [doing painful thing]?`
4. `How to [result] in [timeframe]`
5. `[Name], your [thing] is ready`
6. `Why [number] [founders] switched to [thing]`
7. `Stop [doing thing] (it's hurting your [metric])`
8. `[Trending topic] + what it means for your [thing]`
9. `Get [result] without [pain point]`
10. `[Number] spots left for [offer]`

## Welcome Email Series (5-Email SaaS Onboarding)

**Email 1 — Day 0 (Immediate)**
- Subject: "Welcome to [Product] — start here"
- Content: Thank, set expectations, single quick-win action
- CTA: Complete one core action
- Target: 50-70% open

**Email 2 — Day 1-2**
- Subject: "[Name], here's what [Product] can do for you"
- Content: #1 feature demo with GIF/screenshot
- CTA: "Try [feature] now"
- Target: 40-55% open

**Email 3 — Day 3-4**
- Subject: "How [Customer] achieved [result]"
- Content: Case study + 2-3 pro tips
- CTA: "See how it works"
- Target: 40-55% open

**Email 4 — Day 5-7**
- Subject: "The feature most people miss"
- Content: Underused high-value feature, step-by-step
- CTA: "Unlock this feature"
- Target: 30-45% open

**Email 5 — Day 10-14**
- Subject: "Quick question, [Name]"
- Content: Ask experience, offer help, upgrade path
- CTA: "Reply" or "Book a call"
- Target: 30-45% open

## Email Copy Framework

```
Subject: [30-50 chars, formula above]
Preview: [40-90 chars, complements subject]

1. HOOK (1-2 sentences) — Problem, question, or stat
2. CONTEXT (2-3 sentences) — Why this matters to them
3. VALUE (3-5 sentences) — Insight, tip, story, or offer
4. CTA (1 sentence) — Single clear action

Signature: Name, title, company
```

**Rules:** One benefit per email. Single CTA. Behavior-triggered > time-based (3-5x better).

## Newsletter Structure

1. **Header:** Logo + issue number + mini-TOC
2. **Hero:** Primary story with image
3. **Body:** 2-3 blocks (Headline > 2-3 sentence summary > CTA link)
4. **Secondary:** Tips, tools, curated links
5. **CTA Block:** One primary ask
6. **Footer:** Social links, unsubscribe, address

**Format:** 600px wide, 20px padding, short paragraphs (1-3 sentences), one CTA per section.

## Deliverability Best Practices

**Authentication (required by Gmail/Yahoo):**
- SPF → DKIM → DMARC (rollout: p=none → quarantine → reject)

**Metrics:**
- Inbox placement: 95%+ | Spam complaints: <0.1% | Bounces: <2% | Unsubs: <0.5%

**Rules:**
- Warm new domains: 50-100 emails/day, +20-30% daily over 2-4 weeks
- Send from subdomain (mail.yourdomain.com)
- Clean list quarterly (remove 90-day no-opens)
- 60%+ text, <40% images
- One-click unsubscribe header required
- Consistent schedule builds reputation
- Avoid: "free", "guarantee", "act now", excessive CAPS/exclamation
