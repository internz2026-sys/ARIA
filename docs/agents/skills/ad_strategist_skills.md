# Ad Strategist Skills

## Facebook/Meta Campaign Structure

```
CAMPAIGN (Objective + Budget)
  └── AD SET (Audience + Placement + Schedule)
        └── AD (Creative + Copy + CTA)
```

### 3-Campaign Framework

| Campaign | Purpose | Budget | Audience | Bidding |
|----------|---------|--------|----------|---------|
| Prospecting (TOFU) | Find new users | 60% | Cold: broad interests, LAL | CBO, Lowest Cost |
| Retargeting (MOFU) | Re-engage visitors | 20% | Warm: site visitors, engagers | ABO, Cost Cap |
| Conversion (BOFU) | Close the sale | 20% | Hot: cart abandoners, email list | ABO, Bid Cap |

**CBO vs ABO:**
- ABO for testing (fair budget per ad set)
- CBO for scaling (Meta auto-distributes to winners)
- Workflow: ABO (test) → validate → CBO (scale)

## Audience Targeting

**Cold (60% budget):** Broad interests, Lookalike 1-3%. In 2026, broad targeting outperforms hyper-specific. Min source for LAL: 1,000-50,000 people.

**Warm (20%):** Website visitors 30-180 days, video viewers (25%/50%/75%), page engagers 90-365 days, email subs.

**Hot (20%):** Cart abandoners 7-30 days, lead form submitters, past customers.

**Sizes:** Min 1,000/audience. Retargeting 5K-500K. Broad prospecting 1M+. Warm converts 2-3x more, custom audiences deliver up to 8x ROAS.

## Ad Copy Formulas

### PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solution)
```
[PROBLEM]: Their specific problem in their language
[AGITATE]: Make it urgent — consequences of inaction
[SOLUTION]: Your product as the answer + CTA
```
Best for: Feed ads, pain-point products.

### AIDA (Attention-Interest-Desire-Action)
```
[ATTENTION]: Bold hook — stat, question, or claim
[INTEREST]: Relevant details they care about
[DESIRE]: Paint the outcome after using your product
[ACTION]: Clear CTA with urgency
```
Best for: Landing pages, longer ad copy.

### BAB (Before-After-Bridge)
```
[BEFORE]: Current painful state
[AFTER]: Dream state, problem solved
[BRIDGE]: Your product is the bridge
```
Best for: Transformation products, testimonial-style ads.

## Creative Specs (2026)

| Placement | Dimensions | Ratio | Size |
|-----------|-----------|-------|------|
| Feed Image | 1080x1080 or 1080x1350 | 1:1 or 4:5 | <5MB |
| Feed Video | 1080x1350 | 4:5 | <100MB |
| Stories/Reels | 1080x1920 | 9:16 | <100MB |
| Carousel | 1080x1080 | 1:1 | <5MB/card |

**Text Limits:**
- Primary text: ~125 chars before truncation
- Headline: ~40 chars (~25-30 visible on mobile)
- Description: ~30 chars visible
- Image text: under 20% of area

**Video:** Hook in first 3 seconds. Captions required (85% watched without sound). H.264 MP4/MOV.

**Stories Safe Zones:** Top 14% clear (profile overlay), bottom 20% clear (CTA button).

## Budget Guide

| Tier | Daily | Monthly |
|------|-------|---------|
| Testing | $20-35/ad set | $600-1,050 |
| Small Business | $50-100/day | $1,500-3,000 |
| Growth | $100-300/day | $3,000-9,000 |

**Split:** 70% proven winners, 30% testing.

**Scaling Rules:**
- Increase 10-20%/week after 14+ days at goal ROAS
- Pull back if CPA rises >20% over a week
- Pull back if frequency >3 (cold) or >6 (retargeting)
- Never >20% budget increase per adjustment (avoids resetting learning phase)

## A/B Testing Framework

**Priority:** Creative > Copy > Audience > Placements > Bidding.

**Rules:**
- ONE variable per test
- Min 3-7 days (72 hours minimum)
- Min data: 1,000 impressions or 50 clicks per variant
- Wait for 95% confidence
- Kill at 72 hours if >2x target CPA
- Test 10-12 creatives weekly
- Budget: $20-35/ad set/day, 3-5 ad sets per test
